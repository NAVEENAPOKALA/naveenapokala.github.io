About Me

I am a graduate student and AI/ML Research Assistant at the University of North Carolina at Greensboro, where I design and implement end-to-end machine learning pipelines using PyTorch and large language models, covering data preprocessing, embedding generation, model training, and evaluation.

My research on stock price prediction using annual reports and large language models resulted in a paper accepted at the ACDSA 2026 Conference, demonstrating my ability to apply advanced ML techniques to real-world financial problems.

Alongside my research background, I bring strong software engineering experience building cloud-native microservices, RESTful APIs, and CI/CD pipelines. I value ownership, collaboration, and continuous learning, and I thrive in team-driven engineering environments. I am seeking opportunities where I can combine my software engineering expertise with machine learning to solve impactful real-world problems.
